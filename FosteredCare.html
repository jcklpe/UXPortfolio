Fostered Care


I took part in a charity hackathon in 2014 in which we sought to show ways that technology could make the world a better place. My team won second place for our app Fostered Care. The needs of foster care children are unique, and it's often har for parents of kids to find legally qualified babysisters. The app Fostered Care is meant to help parents find sitters, and sitters find families in need. The eventual scope of the project was hopefully to also help foster parents deal with the many paperwork intricacies and legal requirements placed on them by their organizations, in making their lives easier, also make the lives of the kids easier. Technology can do a lot of good! I'm in particular interested in the potential that it holds in fields like this, as non-profits have tended to be a little behind the curve on some of the recent revolutions in interactive technology.
