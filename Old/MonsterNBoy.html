Monster'n'Boy


          This is one of the first projects I tried to get off the ground coming back from London. Quickly learned how difficult it was to work with a new partner online, who lives in a different timezone. The premise was pretty simple. A basic RPG, much like Mario RPG, with a focus on simplicity and not needing any written words, so as to be maximally accessible. The story was to be about a boy who wakes up in the middle of the night and finds a monster friend. Inspired in MonsterNBoy part by Where the Wild Things Are and Calvin and Hobbes. I wanted it to play with surrealism and blend between the real world and world of monsters. Also you can see my first attempt as animation. They're not very good, but I can do it in a pinch.
