About

     Hi, my name is David French.

      Dynamic, self-motivated designer with a robust toolset of skills in UX, UI, interaction design, typography, video production, motion graphics, illustration, writing, and front end design. I am always refining my craft, and enjoy striving for creative and innovative solutions to whatever puzzle is thrown my way.

      Here’s a little bit about me and my experience. I'm currently working at Lodestone Social as a UX Designer. Before that I worked at KingsIsle Entertainment (a local MMORPG company that serves 45 million users) as a Usability Analyst. I was brought in to work as the lead analyst for their transition from web to mobile, and acted as an assistant producer between departments. Before that I worked as a staff designer for Dream It Ventures, a start up accelerator that was rated as one of the best in the nation by Forbes Magazine. When I was going to Brandeis University I was the lead designer for our school’s multi-media center. I redesigned and maintained our library’s CMS website, redesigned our lab’s HTML/CSS website, and created design materials for print and web for campus outreach.

      While I double majored in Fine Art and Film at Brandeis, my fevor for Interactive Design was kindled while studying at the Slade School of Art in London. After becoming involved with some indie hacker groups in London, I focused my fine arts thesis on interactive design in a fine arts installation context. I did all that while also starting my own student-run media company and doing the most ambitious and extensive film production thesis that had ever been done at my school before. Interactive Design is a fascinating field, dynamic and filled with untapped potential in a vast possibility space. From mobile apps to VR, from Health/Law to play studies, the future is interactive!

      I’m a founding member of the Austin Virtual Reality club, a frequent hackathon participant, and the kind of guy who rambles on about the button layouts of microwaves at parties. I’m always looking for a new challenge, if you have a problem that needs solving, give me a shout.
