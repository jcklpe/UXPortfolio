Android Watch UI


What UX Designer doesn't want to take a crack at designing some wearables? Here's a couple of doodle wireframes I put together. It's interesting to see what we can do with the limitations of the UI. Notice the little black bar at the bottom? How that effects slant and ergonomics is often overlooked. Additionally we're used to the limitatoins of physical watches when thinking about design watch faces, but what can we do that real watches can't? How can wearable tech be more than just watches 2.0?
