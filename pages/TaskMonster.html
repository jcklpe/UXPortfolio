Monster Task


1.jpg

Monster Task was a personal project that I've been working on in my own time. It's basically a To-Do list app meets Tamagotchi. It uses principles of gamification to incentivize users to meet their goals. Completing tasks levels up specific stats for the Pet Monster, eventually leading to it evolving and changing. I think this app would also be a great tool for families or schools, and setting up a web side parental/teacher control that allowed for that assigning of tasks would be really useful.
