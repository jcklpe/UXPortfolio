Lodestone App Comps


While working at Lodestone, I created app comps and video promo stuff. They have these cool geo-located marketing campaigns, in which beacons in a location interact with the app for targeted marketing promotions. Not only can fans watch the game or event from their seats but they can vote, play trivia, win prizes, and participate in other campaigns to make them more involved and integrated to the event experience.
