Lady Jane Branding Experience


Before I knew what interaction design was, I was just a kid doodling in Flash, and painting in Photoshop. Illustration has been an abiding influence on my approach to visual design. The concept of visual language, articulated by Scott McCloud in "Understanding Comics", and the detailed close study that painting and anatomy classes have afforded me, has informed and shaped my approach to design, allowing me to see the visual form not just as an aesthetic component to design, but a functional one, bringing clarity, hieararchy, and information in it's presentation.
