LA Galaxy Fanzone App


This is an app comp I did for the Los Angeles Galaxy soccer team. It's part of their geo-located marketing campaigns, in which beacons in the stadium interact with the app for targeted marketing promotions. Not only can fans watch the game from their seats but they can vote, play trivia, win prizes, and participate in other campaigns to make them more involved and integrated to the team experience. I also created the comp file in Photoshop with a heavy emphasis on Smart Objects, in order to cut future edits. This makes the file larger but also extremely easy to edit. Change one button, you change all instances of the button across all app screens.
